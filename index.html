Progress Tracker Mobile brings the functionality of App Academy's internal
student/instructor accountability application, to your mobile device.

Built on React Native, PTM allows students to easily authenticate into the student database to access assessment scores, view daily paring and classmate information, as well as, automated check-in.

OAuth 2.0

PT Mobile uses Github OAuth as well as resetting session tokens to keep student accounts secure and active sessions feeling smooth.

Automated Check-In

When given location permission on their mobile device, PT Mobile allows students to check-in automatically when on-site at the campus.

Advanced Statistics

Progress Tracker Mobile de-clutters the legacy visualization and provides a cleaner and more intuitive overview of all student assessments.

Seize the Day

Progress Tracker Mobile clears the confusion of who the day's pair is and where they'll be located. With instant authentication and automated check-in, finding your pair can't get any easier.

Why

As App Academy graduates, any and all assistance we can provide to foster the growth of talented developers is an honor and tradition. Overcoming pain points is a pillar of software development. So we addressed both.
